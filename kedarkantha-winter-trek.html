Your Private Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Dehradun to Sankri (6,455 ft)

Private vehicle transfer through scenic mountain roads. Evening cultural welcome at our luxury basecamp with traditional Garhwali dinner.

Day 2: Sankri to Juda Ka Talab (9,100 ft)

Trek through ancient pine and oak forests. Luxury camping by the frozen lake with gourmet dinner and stargazing session.

Day 3: Juda Ka Talab to KK Base (11,250 ft)

Ascend through snow-covered trails with stunning valley views. Evening acclimatization walks and photography sessions.

Day 4: Summit Day (12,500 ft)

Early morning summit push with professional guides. Panoramic views of Himalayan peaks. Celebration dinner at base camp.

Day 5: Base to Sankri

Leisurely descent with photography stops. Evening spa treatment and farewell dinner at Sankri.

Day 6: Sankri to Dehradun

Morning yoga session followed by comfortable return journey to Dehradun.
