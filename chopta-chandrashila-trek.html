Your Private Trek Journey

Day 1: Rishikesh to Chopta (8,790 ft)

Luxury vehicle transfer through scenic Himalayan roads. Evening arrival at our exclusive Chopta campsite with welcome ceremony and gourmet dinner under the stars.

Day 2: Acclimatization & Local Exploration

Gentle nature walks, bird watching, and photography sessions. Evening meditation and yoga at camp with stunning sunset views.

Day 3: Chopta to Tungnath Temple (12,073 ft)

Private guided trek to the highest Shiva temple. Exclusive puja ceremony and spiritual discourse. Luxury camping with panoramic views.

Day 4: Chandrashila Summit (13,123 ft)

Early morning summit climb for spectacular sunrise. Meditation session at peak. Afternoon descent with photography stops. Celebration dinner at Chopta.

Day 5: Chopta to Rishikesh

Morning yoga and breakfast. Comfortable return journey with stops at local attractions and markets.
